Apr 21, 2026 Researcher Jorge A. Acuña has published a new article in collaboration with colleagues from the Universitiy of South Florida (USF), “Introducing a Sequential Machine Learning Framework to Predict ICU Admission and Hospital Length of Stay Using Admission-time Data”. The study highlights how incorporating intermediate ICU-related predictions improves overall accuracy and provides actionable insights for hospital resource planning and patient flow management, available at https://doi.org/10.1177/20552076261443021.
