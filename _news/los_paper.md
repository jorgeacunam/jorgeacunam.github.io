Researcher Jorge A. Acuña has published a new article in collaboration with colleagues from the [University of South Florida (USF)](www.usf.edu), introducing a sequential machine learning framework to predict ICU admission and hospital length of stay using admission-time data. The study highlights how incorporating intermediate ICU-related predictions improves overall accuracy and provides actionable insights for hospital resource planning and patient flow management, available at [https://doi.org/10.1177/20552076261443021](https://doi.org/10.1177/20552076261443021).
