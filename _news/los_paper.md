Researcher Jorge A. Acuña has published a new article in collaboration with colleagues from [USF](https://www.usf.edu), "Introducing a Sequential Machine Learning Framework to Predict ICU Admission and Hospital Length of Stay Using Admission-time Data". The study highlights how incorporating intermediate ICU-related predictions improves overall accuracy and provides actionable insights for hospital resource planning and patient flow management, available at [https://doi.org/10.1177/20552076261443021](https://doi.org/10.1177/20552076261443021).
