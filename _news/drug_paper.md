Researcher Jorge A. Acuña has published a new article in collaboration with Kevin Palomino in Health Care Management Science, “Bi-objective location-allocation model of interventions in high drug consumption areas incorporating X topic modeling”. The study proposes an optimization framework that integrates social media sentiment analysis and topic modeling to support the location and allocation of prevention and mitigation services in high drug-consumption areas, balancing risk reduction, geographic accessibility, and equity under budget constraints. The article is available at [https://doi.org/10.1007/s10729-025-09753-3](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10729-025-09753-3).
